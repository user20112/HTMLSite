Contest: The Fellowship Of The Ring 2002

Welcome To The Fourth NeHe Productions OpenGL Challenge!



The demos for this contest had to have something to do with The Lord Of The Rings - The Fellowship of the Ring (the first movie or book). Not books 2 or 3! The demos had to include source code, and had to compile without any major problems. The winner was decided based on visitor feedback, and feedback from a few close friends of mine.

The prize for this contest was an NVIDIA GeForce4 Ti 4600 (fully loaded... TV out / in, etc). At the time this contest was announced, the Ti 4600 had just been released and not many people could afford one, let alone get their hands on one. I had to special order the card at just over $800.
