Enrique C. Basadre Stevenson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Enrique C. Basadre Stevenson (1848 – February 22, 1925) was a Peruvian physician and politician. He was born in Tacna, Peru. He graduated from the University of San Marcos. He served as minister of the interior (November 1910 – August 1911) in the Government of Peru. He was Prime Minister of Peru (December 1910 – August 31, 1911) under President Augusto B. Leguía y Salcedo. He was a member of the Senate of Peru (1920–1925). He died in Lima.
